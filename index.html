OUR SERVICES

Business Health Check

We do a deep review of your business operations, finances and tax status. We look out for opportunities to mitigate your risks, optimise your processes and increase your value. The output will be prioritized recommendations for the management team. We can also offer fractional CFO services to assist in implementation

Strategy

We help build your overall corporate strategy. Where one already exists, we will support in building the supporting finance and operational strategy and help in its review and update

Fractional CFO Services

We come into your business as a strategic finance partner to help you achieve your full finance potential. We take on strategic finance initiatives including internal business investor readiness, recommendations from finance health check and upskilling your current team and finance processes

Fundraising & Investor readiness

Where fundraising is required, we will leverage on our networks to identify potential funders. We will help you develop your investor documents, support in due diligence and engage with funders until funding is secured

Corporate Finance

We review your operations to identify working opportunities to better leverage your working capital. We review your financial accounts and projections against your strategy and advise on the most optimal capital structure.
